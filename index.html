About Us

With a legacy spanning over four decades, we are proud to be one of the oldest property in Saputara. Nestled close to the serene lake, we offer an oasis of tranquility and comfort. Known for our warm hospitality and affordable rates, we have become the preferred choice for travelers seeking both relaxation and adventure in the heart of nature.

Miss Pramilaben K. Sualy laid the foundation of this property in 1996 with deep emotion and passion, aiming to create a uniquely designed structure that stands as an architectural beauty—small yet exquisite for comfortable living. Her vision not only shaped this remarkable space but also served as an inspiration for women's empowerment while extending warm hospitality to tourists.
